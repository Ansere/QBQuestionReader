Welcome to UGSBowl!

This is a website where you can complete with your friends to see who can identify quotes from Professor Daniel Bonevac's UGS 303: Ideas of the Twentieth Century course the fastest. It is like a gameshow, where you need to buzz in and type your answer in order to get points for the question. However, misidentifying the author incurs a penalty, so be careful.

Controls

Press N to start the question, SPACE to buzz, and ENTER to chat.
